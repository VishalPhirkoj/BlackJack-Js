RULES FOR BLACKJACK CARD GAME

1. This is card game (BlackJack) based on standard 52-card pack

2. Each participant attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

3. It is up to each individual player if an ace is worth 1 or 11. Face cards are 10 and any other card is its pip value.

4. Before the deal begins, each player places a bet, in chips, in front of them in the designated area.

5. Minimum and maximum limits are established on the betting, and the general limits are from ₹2 to ₹500.
